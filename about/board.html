Board Meetings

The Board of Directors meets the 4th Wednesday of August, October, January, March and June at 5:30pm.

The Finance Committee meets the 3rd Tuesday of every month at 5:30pm.

The Governance Committee will meet 11/7/17, 1/16/18, 3/7/18, 7/17/18, 9/18/18, and 11/13/18 at 3pm.

The Executive Committee will meet the 3rd Tuesday of Sept, November, February, April, and July at 9am.

All meetings are held at Prodeo Academy.

The upcoming agenda, all board minutes, most recent audited financial statements, policies, and our Authorizer's Annual Report can be viewed on Google Drive.

Minnesota law requires that all chartered schools be authorized by an organization approved by the commissioner of education to be an authorizer.

Prodeo Academy's authorizer is Innovative Quality Schools (IQS) which was approved as an authorizer on December 1, 2010. Contact IQS:
