Volunteer

If you are inspired to help close the achievement gap for our students, join us as a volunteer.

You have opportunities throughout the day Monday through Friday:

  • Tutoring our students in small groups for 1 1/2 hours one to four days per week. Help our students learn letters, numbers, and colors during tutoring sessions Monday - Thursday from 8:35 - 10:35.
  • Helping to serve lunch from 11:10 AM - 1:10 PM one to five days per week.
  • Riding the bus home with our students for 30-45 minutes at 4:00 PM one to five days per week.
  • Providing Administrative Assistance support on a flexible schedule throughout the week.

If you have time and talent to share, please contact Rick Campion, Executive Director.
