Power Up Prodeo: A Fundraising Party for Our Scholars is on Thursday, May 3rd from 5:00-9:00pm.


This year, it will be held at the Minneapolis Event Center.

Join us at 5PM for networking cocktail hour, featuring heavy apps throughout multiple food stations and bars. 

At 6:30PM, we will have a short 30 minute program, including a video featuring Prodeo teachers. Afterwards, people are welcome to network and mingle again in the bar area.

Table Sponsorships are available. For more information, please contact Laura Durden at 612-524-8649.  

Tickets are $85.00. Visit https://tinyurl.com/powerupprodeo to register!

Support Prodeo scholars and be part of closing the achievement gap in Minnesota.

We hope to see you there!
