Community Meetings

Every Friday, the students and staff gather in the gym from 12:20pm-12:50pm for Community Meeting to celebrate the week.

Parents and visitors are always welcome to attend.

Spirit Stick

The highlight of the meeting is the awarding of the Spirit Stick. It is awarded to the student whose actions during the week best embodied the Prodeo Academy values.

This scholar proudly keeps the Spirit Stick in his or her classroom during the week and passes it on at the next Community Meeting.
